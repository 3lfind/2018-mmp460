Part 3A

This article introduces the 6 steps to help people prepare a great online portfolio.

First, take a look all of your work, and choose the best pieces which should be the type of work you want to do in the future for your portfolio. And, your portfolio should include at least five projects with good quality. Second, make your project to be quick reading with a title, short paragraph, and engaging image. Third, make your website more straightforward and simple to let your portfolio stand out. Fourth, create a bio to tell your story which can share your points of view and experience. Fifth, giving some distinctive elements for your website like contact and blog. Finally, keep updating your work to make your portfolio fresh.
